The Blue Lagoon

A paradise of crystal-clear turquoise waters nestled on the peaceful island of Comino.

Comino's Natural Masterpiece

📍 Comino Island, Malta

The Blue Lagoon is widely recognized as one of Malta's most breathtaking natural treasures. Situated between the small island of Comino and the islet of Cominotto, this giant natural pool features a white sandy bottom and exceptionally clear, glowing cyan waters that resemble a tropical paradise in the middle of the Mediterranean Sea.

It is a top destination for travelers looking to swim, snorkel, or cruise by boat. Surrounded by scenic limestone cliffs and small caves, the lagoon offers an unforgettable view. To fully experience its peaceful atmosphere, visiting early in the morning or during a sunset boat trip allows you to enjoy the golden Maltese sun reflecting beautifully across the turquoise sea.
