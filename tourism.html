The Blue Lagoon

A paradise of crystal-clear turquoise waters nestled on the peaceful island of Comino.

Comino's Natural Masterpiece

📍 Comino Island, Malta

The Blue Lagoon is widely recognized as one of Malta's most breathtaking natural treasures. Situated between the small island of Comino and the islet of Cominotto, this giant natural pool features a white sandy seafloor that reflects the sunlight to create a glowing cyan surface, making it one of the top locations in Europe for swimming and snorkeling.

Historically, Comino island served as a key hiding spot for pirates and smugglers during the Middle Ages due to its secluded bays and complex cave systems. Today, it offers an unforgettable getaway where visitors can relax under the golden Mediterranean sun.
